Why to get a container home

Container homes are about living cozy, freely and debt free. The homes can be built fast and for an affordable price. It is a trend to live as simple as you can without a big home, while still adding a twist of style to your tiny home. They are eco friendly and good for the environment. Go buy your container now while prices may go up if you wait.

Things to know before building

  • Not all containers are the same length
  • Prices can vary by condition and size
  • Make sure to research building regulations
  • Have a building plan and layout
  • It is best to find people who sell the containers locally
  • Make count of materiels you will be using
  • Do your research
